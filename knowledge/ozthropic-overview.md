# Ozthropic Overview

Ozthropic builds human-centric AI systems for Australian small and medium-sized businesses.

The company focuses on practical automation for:

- AI phone agents
- Booking and CRM workflows
- Email triage and draft replies
- Quoting and invoicing workflows
- Social and advertising support
- Strategy, optimisation, and ongoing maintenance

Ozthropic's promise:

- Affordable to start
- Fair to stay
- Built around the humans who use it
